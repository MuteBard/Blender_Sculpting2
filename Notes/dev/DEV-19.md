## Snapping against Faces

![](../images/DEV-19/DEV-19-B1.png)

    Make sure your element is rotated correctly, it depends on the origin point of the shape.
    Apply the rotation
    Then G to move
    Hold CTRL
    Then drag against the mesh until aligned
